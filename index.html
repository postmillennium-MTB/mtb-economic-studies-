Data sources & methodology notes
Visitor spending: TPL/IMBA Economic Benefits of Mountain Biking (2025), 50-study meta-analysis. Conservative $103/trip = Duluth, MN; moderate $175/trip = regional avg; optimistic $416/trip = TPL national avg.

Property value premiums: Crompton & Nicholls (2019); NAR Field Guide (2020). Typical: 3–5%. Indianapolis Cultural Trail: $1B added within 500 ft, 2008–2014.

Trail build & maintenance costs: Singletracks.com contractor survey (Apr 2026); American Trails database ($1,500–$5,000/mi/yr).

Grants: RTP (Recreational Trails Program) funds up to 80% of trail construction costs. RAISE, CMAQ, and state-level programs also apply. Grant percentage reduces the municipal capital contribution used in breakeven and ROI calculations; total project cost is unchanged.

Color accessibility: The breakeven chart uses charcoal (#555, dashed) for costs and green (#1A8C3E, with markers) for revenue — distinguishable under deuteranopia and protanopia.

Breakeven: Year when cumulative municipal revenue (sales tax + property tax) ≥ municipal share of construction cost (after grants). Maintenance excluded from breakeven denominator.

This tool is for planning and advocacy. Validate with local data before formal presentation. Investments over $1M should commission a formal IMPLAN or REMI study.
